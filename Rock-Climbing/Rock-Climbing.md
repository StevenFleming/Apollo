#### Rock Climbing is a wonderful way to connect with nature and your body
The benefits of rock climbing include
* strong core
* strong hands
* friendly community 
* nature oriented activities 
* low start up cost
* group activity
* scenic trips 
* good workout, lots of fun
* push limits

# Climbing Equipment
